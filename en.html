Safety that fits you

SafeNow is the safety app for your everyday life. We're looking for people who want to help shape it on a regular basis. Nobody knows better than you what safety means in everyday life. Your experience helps us build safety that truly fits you.

What does participating mean

The Lead User Panel is a group of people who regularly help shape SafeNow.

We invite you to surveys, tests, or conversations. You decide each time whether and how you want to participate. During sign-up, you simply select what works for you — for example just surveys or also conversations. We'll only reach out for those. No fixed schedule, no pressure.

Your pace, your way

From 5 minutes to coffee at the office

Every form of participation helps us. You choose what works for you.

📝

Online surveys

5-10 min

A short questionnaire, whenever and wherever suits you. On your phone, laptop, from the couch. Perfect for in between.

💻

Unmoderated tests

10-20 min

You get a prototype and try it out at your own pace, with nobody watching.

🎤

One-on-one conversations

30-45 min

A relaxed conversation via video call with someone from our team. You talk, we listen. No test, just an honest exchange.

🏢

In person in Munich

45-90 min

Meet the team in person, test new features at our office, and chat with us over coffee.

📱

Test new features

varies

Get access to new features before they go live. Just use them in your daily life and tell us how they feel.

🗣

Focus groups

60-90 min

A small group of 4-6 people discusses a topic together via video. Different perspectives, often the best ideas.
